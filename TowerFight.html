Tower Fight Rules

Welcome to Tower Fight! Here's how to play:

  • Use arrow keys to move your player.
  • Press "F" to shoot bullets at the enemy and enemy generators.
  • Destroy enemy energy generators to temporarily disable their shooting.
  • Your shooting is disabled for 10 seconds when one generator is damaged.
  • If both your generators are destroyed, healing is disabled until restored.
  • Defend your castle and bridge while attacking the enemy.
  • Player heals only inside your castle if generators are alive.
  • The game starts after the 15-second countdown.
